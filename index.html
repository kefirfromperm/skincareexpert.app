Skincare Expert

Your personal cosmetologist

Skincare Expert is a new modern way to obtain skincare which exactly suitable for you. The application is developed in collaboration with professional cosmetologists and provide best service for searching skincare products.

Skincare Expert pays attention for your skin type and state, problems, age, diseases, climate, indoor climate etc. Based on these the application develops a list of recommendations especially for you. If you don't confident about you problems it is not a problem because Skincare Expert allows you to specify any parameters in a lightweight visualised way. After that you give a list of recommended skincare products by categories and are able to buy it on the popular beauty shops.
